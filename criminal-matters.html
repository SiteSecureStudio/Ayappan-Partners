Our Process

How We Approach
Criminal Defence

01
Immediate Consultation
We meet with you promptly, review the charges and evidence, and advise on your options clearly and honestly.
02
Bail & Preliminary Hearings
We appear at all mention dates, apply for bail where appropriate, and manage all procedural steps diligently.
03
Trial Preparation
We challenge the prosecution's evidence rigorously, identify weaknesses, and prepare your defence comprehensively.
