Overview

Defence That
Matters Most

When your liberty is at stake, you need a counsel who is prepared, present, and experienced. Ayappan & Partners provides criminal defence representation across a range of offences, at all court levels from Magistrates' Court through to appellate proceedings.

Principal counsel has appeared in reported criminal decisions, including Azmir Mirza Mahmud v PP [2025] CLJU 1885 at the High Court of Temerloh. We bring the same rigorous preparation to every criminal brief regardless of complexity.

We believe every accused person is entitled to the best possible defence. Our approach is thorough, frank, and focused on achieving the best outcome within the law.

Our Process

How We Approach
Criminal Defence
