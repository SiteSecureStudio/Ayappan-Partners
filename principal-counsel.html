Counsel Profile

An Advocate Built on
Depth of Knowledge

Rajan Ayappan is the principal counsel of Ayappan & Partners, called to the Malaysian Bar and admitted as an Advocate & Solicitor of the High Court of Malaya on 30 April 2004. He read law as a pupil in the chambers of Muralee Menon at Messrs Jaffar & Menon, a distinguished firm known for its courtroom excellence.

His core areas of expertise span insurance litigation (motor & non-motor), clinical negligence, probate & administration, land disputes, and conveyancing. He is widely recognised for his work in these fields and has secured landmark decisions before the Federal Court and Court of Appeal.

The firm currently serves as Ad hoc Solicitor for the Naza Group of Companies, having successfully completed transactions including a joint venture agreement valued at RM 15.4 million and a sale & purchase agreement for a building valued at RM 8 million.

Rajan Ayappan believes in direct, senior-level involvement in every matter — clients speak to principal counsel, not intermediaries. This commitment to personal representation has defined the firm's reputation since 2009.

Expertise

Core Areas of
Specialisation

Insurance Litigation

Motor and non-motor insurance disputes, including bodily injury claims, fatal claims, subrogation actions, fraud, and declaration suits. Extensive track record at the Court of Appeal and Federal Court.

Clinical Negligence

Medical and clinical negligence claims representing both plaintiffs and defendants. Deep expertise in the procedural and evidential requirements unique to medical liability actions.

Civil Litigation

Full spectrum civil litigation including contractual disputes, injunctions (Anton Pillar, Mareva), land disputes, goods sold and delivered, and bankruptcy proceedings.

Probate & Administration

Estate administration, letters of administration, grants of probate, and contested estate matters. Efficient handling with sensitivity to family circumstances.

Conveyancing

Full property conveyancing services including sub-sales, discharge of charge, assignment, and high-value commercial transactions for corporate clients including the Naza Group.

Criminal Defence

Criminal defence representation including drug-related offences, traffic offences under the Road Transport Act, and general criminal defence at all court levels.
