Overview

Insurance Expertise with
Proven Results

Insurance litigation is a core strength of Ayappan & Partners. With a track record that includes a landmark Federal Court decision and two reported Court of Appeal cases, we bring substantive expertise to every insurance matter.

We represent insurers, insureds, and claimants in motor and non-motor insurance disputes. Our deep familiarity with the Road Transport Act, policy interpretation, and the procedural requirements of insurance litigation gives our clients a decisive advantage.

Our landmark Federal Court decision — AmGeneral Insurance Bhd v Sa'Amran a/l Atan & Ors [2022] 8 CLJ 175 — is widely cited as authority on motor insurance liability in Malaysia.

Our Process

How We Approach
Insurance Disputes
