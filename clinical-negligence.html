Clinical Negligence

Dedicated expertise in medical and clinical negligence claims — protecting patient rights and holding healthcare providers accountable.

Overview

Accountability in
Medical Care

Clinical negligence claims are among the most complex and sensitive areas of law. They require not only a deep understanding of legal principles but also the ability to analyse medical evidence, engage expert witnesses, and navigate the procedural demands of medical liability litigation.

Ayappan & Partners brings both the legal expertise and the practical experience to handle clinical negligence matters. Principal counsel has managed cases involving surgical errors, delayed diagnosis, medication errors, and hospital liability — always approaching each matter with the seriousness it deserves.

We work with clients to understand what went wrong, secure appropriate expert medical opinions, and pursue just compensation through the courts.

Our Process

How We Build
Your Claim

01
Case Evaluation
We review your medical records, identify potential breach of duty, and advise whether your case has reasonable prospects.
02
Expert Engagement
We retain appropriate medical experts to provide independent opinions on the standard of care and causation.
03
Letter of Demand
We issue a formal letter of demand to the responsible healthcare provider, often triggering early settlement discussions.
04
Litigation or Settlement
We file suit if settlement is not achieved and pursue the matter to trial, with principal counsel appearing personally.
