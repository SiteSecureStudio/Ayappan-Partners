Overview

Estate Administration
with Sensitivity

Dealing with the estate of a loved one is a difficult time. Ayappan & Partners provides efficient, compassionate guidance through the probate and administration process, ensuring estates are wound up correctly and beneficiaries receive what they are entitled to.

Whether there is a valid will in place or the deceased passed without one, we assist executors and administrators through every step of the process — from filing the petition to distributing the estate assets.

We also advise on contested estates and disputed wills where family members disagree on entitlements or the validity of the deceased's wishes.

Our Process

How We Administer
an Estate

01
Document Gathering
We identify and compile all relevant documents — the will (if any), death certificate, asset records, and beneficiary details.
02
Court Petition
We prepare and file the petition for Grant of Probate or Letters of Administration at the appropriate High Court registry.
03
Estate Valuation
We assist in identifying, valuing, and securing all estate assets including property, bank accounts, and investments.
04
Distribution
We oversee the payment of debts and distribution of remaining assets to beneficiaries in accordance with the will or law.
