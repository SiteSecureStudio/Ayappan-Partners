Introduction

A Firm Built on
Trust & Precision

Ayappan & Partners (formerly known as Messrs. Rajan, Gurjit & Associates) was established in 2009 and is located in the heart of Sentul, Kuala Lumpur. Our experience spans civil & criminal litigation, insurance litigation, clinical negligence, conveyancing, and more — at all court levels.

We serve as Ad hoc Solicitor for the Naza Group of Companies, and our primary objective is to deliver top-quality, professional legal services that are reliable, responsive, and cost-effective.

Counsel Profile

An Advocate Built on
Depth of Knowledge

Rajan Ayappan is the principal counsel, called to the Malaysian Bar and admitted as an Advocate & Solicitor of the High Court of Malaya on 30 April 2004. With over 20 years of practice, his expertise spans insurance litigation, clinical negligence, probate, and conveyancing.

He has secured landmark decisions before the Federal Court and Court of Appeal, and holds a Master's of Law in Business Law from the International Islamic University Malaysia.
