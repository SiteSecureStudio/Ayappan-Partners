Introduction

A Firm Built on
Trust & Precision

Ayappan & Partners (formerly known as Messrs. Rajan, Gurjit & Associates) was established in 2009 and is located in the heart of Sentul, Kuala Lumpur — a fast-developing business hub.

Our experience and expertise include handling civil & criminal litigation, professional negligence, corporate & commercial litigation, insurance litigation, drug-related offences, and other criminal matters — in courts at all levels, including appellate courts.

We represent both corporate and individual clients, providing litigation expertise and undertaking transactional work across a broad spectrum of the law. Our firm's primary objective is to deliver top-quality, professional legal services that are reliable, responsive, and cost-effective.

The firm currently serves as Ad hoc Solicitor for the Naza Group of Companies and has successfully represented them in numerous matters.

Counsel Profile

An Advocate Built on
Depth of Knowledge

Rajan Ayappan is the principal counsel of Ayappan & Partners, called to the Malaysian Bar and admitted as an Advocate & Solicitor of the High Court of Malaya on 30 April 2004. He read law as a pupil in the chambers of Muralee Menon at Messrs Jaffar & Menon.

His core areas of expertise span insurance litigation (motor & non-motor), clinical negligence, probate & administration, land disputes, and conveyancing. He is widely recognised for work in these fields and has secured landmark decisions before the Federal Court and Court of Appeal.

The firm currently serves as Ad hoc Solicitor for the Naza Group of Companies, having successfully completed transactions including a joint venture agreement valued at RM 15.4 million and a sale & purchase agreement for a building valued at RM 8 million.
