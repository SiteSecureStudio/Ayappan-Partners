Corporate & Commercial

Overview

Commercial Law for
Serious Business

Ayappan & Partners advises and represents corporate clients in commercial disputes and transactions. We understand that businesses need lawyers who are responsive, commercially aware, and capable of resolving disputes efficiently — in the boardroom or the courtroom.

Our corporate work includes representation of the Naza Group of Companies as Ad hoc Solicitor, successfully completing a joint venture agreement valued at RM 15.4 million and a sale & purchase transaction for a building valued at RM 8 million.

We bring the same rigour to smaller commercial disputes and advisory mandates — because every commercial matter matters to the business that faces it.
