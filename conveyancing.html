Overview

Precise Property
Transactions

Property transactions represent significant financial commitments. Ayappan & Partners provides thorough, timely conveyancing services to ensure your transaction proceeds smoothly and your interests are protected throughout.

We handle both residential and high-value commercial transactions. Our work for the Naza Group of Companies — including a joint venture agreement at RM 15.4 million and a sale & purchase agreement for a building valued at RM 8 million — demonstrates our capacity for complex, large-scale property work.

Whether you are a first-time homebuyer or a corporate entity executing a multi-million ringgit transaction, we apply the same precision and attention to every matter.

Our Process

How We Handle
Your Transaction

01
Initial Instructions
We take your instructions, review the offer letter or heads of terms, and conduct title and encumbrance searches.
02
Agreement Preparation
We draft or review the Sale & Purchase Agreement, ensuring your interests and obligations are properly protected.
03
Loan & Charge Documents
We liaise with the bank's panel solicitors, prepare loan documentation, and coordinate for charge registration.
04
Completion & Registration
We ensure timely completion, attend to stamping, and perfect the title transfer and charge at the Land Registry.
